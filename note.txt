for ordering                        -->localhost:8000/api/users?order[id]=DESC
                                    -->localhost:8000/api/users?order[id]=ASC
for range based selection           -->localhost:8000/api/users?range=[{"property":"id","lower":11,"upper":12}]&order[id]=DESC
for multiple range based selection  -->localhost:8000/api/users?range=[{"property":"id","lower":11,"upper":12},{"property":"name","lower":"j","upper":"k"}]
for pagination                      -->localhost:8000/api/users?skip=1&take=1
                                        skip = no of items to skip and take = no of items to take after skip 
for where                           -->localhost:8000/api/users?name=Amrit
